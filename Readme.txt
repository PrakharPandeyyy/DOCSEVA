   doctors in your area. Manage your healthcare with ease through our user-friendly interface.

   confidentiality. We prioritize the security and privacy of your sensitive information.

🌟 How to Get Started:

To start using DocSeva, follow these simple steps:

1. Sign Up: Create your account on our website or mobile app. It's quick, easy, and free!

2. Browse Doctors: Explore our extensive list of certified doctors and specialists. Read
   their profiles and reviews to find the perfect match for your healthcare needs.

3. Book an Appointment: Choose your preferred doctor and schedule an appointment at a
   convenient date and time.

4. Video Consultation: On the appointment day, access your video call with the doctor
   through the DocSeva platform. Prepare your questions and concerns beforehand.

5. Meet MediBot: Don't forget to chat with our friendly AI assistant. MediBot can assist
   you with general health information and answer frequently asked questions.
